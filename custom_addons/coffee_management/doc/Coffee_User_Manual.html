Level 5: Boss Battles ‚Äì FAQ & Troubleshooting ‚öîÔ∏èüõ°Ô∏è

Quest Objective: Defeat common glitches. Use this as your potion kit!

  • Q: No Manufacturing Order on contract confirm?
    A: Check: Selling finished product? BoM defined? "Manufacture" & "MTO" routes enabled? Fix and retry!
  • Q: Negative stock balance?
    A: Product type must be "Storable" ‚Äì not Consumable. Update in Inventory.
  • Q: Can't select supplier/product?
    A: Suppliers need a Purchase Order first. Products need "Is a Coffee Product" checked.
  • Q: System slow during peaks?
    A: Schedule reports off-peak, boost server resources, use batch mode.
  • Q: Fix wrong quality eval?
    A: Cancel receiving, reset to Draft, re-eval, redo workflow.
